About Us

The Department of Radiology provides diagnostic imaging services to patients of all age groups, from newborn to geriatric, inpatient as well as ambulatory patients. The department is open 24 hours a day, seven days a week, and is under the direct supervision of a board-certified radiologist who works closely with the specially trained radiologic technologists in performing each imaging procedure. The department is staffed with registered technologists, many of whom are registered in multiple imaging modalities.For our medical and allied health students we provide multiple avenues of exposure to and education in Radiology view medical student resources now. We are also very active in first and second year medical student education.
